General Questions

What is CompareHub?

CompareHub is a comprehensive price comparison platform that helps users find the best deals on electronics and tech products across South Africa. We compare prices from multiple retailers to help you make informed purchasing decisions.

Is CompareHub free to use?

Yes, CompareHub is completely free for all users. There are no subscription fees or hidden costs for using our price comparison service.

We are able to offer our service for free because we may earn a commission from retailers when you click through our site and make a purchase. This doesn't affect the price you pay, and it helps us continue to provide and improve our comparison service.

Which retailers do you compare?

We compare prices from major retailers across South Africa, including popular electronics stores, online marketplaces, and specialized tech retailers. Our database is constantly updated to include new retailers and ensure comprehensive coverage.

Do you sell products directly?

No, CompareHub does not sell products directly. We are a price comparison service that helps you find the best deals across different retailers.

When you find a product you want to purchase, we'll direct you to the retailer's website where you can complete your purchase directly with them. We don't process any transactions or handle any payments ourselves.

Account Questions

Do I need to create an account to use CompareHub?

No, you can browse and compare prices without creating an account. However, creating an account allows you to save products to your wishlist, set price alerts, and access additional features.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Login" button in the top navigation, then select "Register" or "Sign Up". You'll need to provide your name and email address, and create a secure password.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click "Login" and then "Forgot Password". Enter your email address and we'll send you a link to reset your password.

Price Comparison Questions

How often are prices updated?

We update our price database multiple times daily to ensure you have access to the most current pricing information. However, prices can change frequently, so we recommend checking the retailer's website for the most up-to-date pricing.

How do you compare prices?

We use automated systems to collect pricing information from retailer websites and databases. Our algorithms match products based on specifications, model numbers, and other identifying features to ensure accurate comparisons.

Can I set price alerts?

Yes! When you create an account, you can set price alerts for specific products. We'll notify you via email when the price drops to your target amount.

Why do prices sometimes differ from the retailer's website?

Prices can change frequently throughout the day. While we update our database regularly, there may be brief periods where the price on the retailer's website differs from what we show. Always check the retailer's website for the final price before purchasing.

Wishlist Questions

How do I add products to my wishlist?

To add a product to your wishlist, simply click the heart icon next to any product. You'll need to be logged in to your account to use this feature.

How do I view my wishlist?

You can access your wishlist by clicking on your profile menu and selecting "Wishlist", or by clicking the heart icon in the navigation bar.

Is there a limit to how many products I can add to my wishlist?

No, there's no limit to the number of products you can add to your wishlist. You can save as many products as you'd like for future reference.

Technical Questions

Which browsers are supported?

CompareHub works with all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. We recommend using the latest version of your preferred browser for the best experience.

Is CompareHub mobile-friendly?

Yes! CompareHub is fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices. You can access all features and compare prices on your smartphone or tablet.

What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?

If you experience any technical issues, please try refreshing the page first. If the problem persists, contact our support team with details about the issue, including your browser type and any error messages you see.

Didn't Find Your Answer?

If you couldn't find the information you were looking for, our support team is here to help.
